COVID

It has been exciting to see people get vaccinated and COVID numbers go down. As New York and other states continue to vaccinate people at a rapid pace, many restrictions have been relaxed (and this page keeps getting shorter!). We are doing our best to follow all CDC and NYS guidelines for weddings and large gatherings. To help keep everyone safe, our ceremony will (hopefully) be outdoors, and our reception is in a large, open barn with ample airflow.


In accordance with NYS and the CDC, vaccinated individuals are no longer required to wear a mask or social distance. We still expect everyone to perform a self-screening for COVID symptoms.


We are encouraging those who are unvaccinated to get tested and/or wear a mask in the barn at our wedding. There are many people attending our wedding (mainly children) who are unable to get vaccinated, and we would appreciate your help in protecting them.
