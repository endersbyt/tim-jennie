FAQs

 

What about COVID?

We recognize the seriousness of the COVID-19 pandemic and want to make sure that we are able to celebrate in a safe way. Check out our COVID page for more info on how COVID affects our wedding. 


Where should I stay?

We have a block of rooms reserved at Embassy Suites by Hilton Syracuse, you can reserve a room in that block here. If you’re looking for something different, there are multiple other hotels in that same area, just look up Carrier Circle on Google Maps and you’ll find a bunch. There are a limited number of Airbnb’s around and the Hilton hotel in Cazenovia may have some availability as well.


Can I use my phone at the ceremony?

We would love it if you would turn off your phone/camera and enjoy the moment with us. We have paid a professional photographer to document our special day, and we would appreciate it if her photos didn’t include your phone. Feel free to take as many photos as you’d like once the ceremony is over!


What’s the dress code?

Summer casual. Our wedding will be outdoors and we’ll have lots of fun activities. We don’t want you to sweat through a suit!


Can I bring a plus one?

Unfortunately, due to COVID regulations we have very strict limits on the number of guests we can have. Since every “plus one” means another one of our friends we can’t invite, we ask that you respect the number of seats we have reserved for you as indicated on your invitation.


Are kids welcome?

If you know anything about Tim’s family, a good 75% of them couldn’t come if kids weren’t invited. Also, we love kids! Bring the little ones! There will be a playground, slides, and other fun activities for them. Your kids should have be included on your invitation. 


Where are the ceremony and the reception taking place?

Both will be at Springside Farm - a Christmas Tree farm in Fabius, NY. The ceremony will be outside, and the reception will be inside their beautiful barn (with very open walls!).


Are there any local places you’d recommend I check out while I’m in town?

If you’re looking for something indoors, you could check out the incredible mall, Destiny USA, or Turning Stone Casino. Otherwise, some of our personal favorite parks include Green Lakes, Chittenango Falls (in Jennie’s hometown!), or Highland Forest!


How early should I arrive?

The ceremony starts at 3:30pm. We will likely be using the space for photos and a short rehearsal before the wedding. Please do not arrive more than 45 minutes ahead of time. Additionally, please arrive at least 15 minutes before the wedding.


Where do I park?

Springside has plenty of parking right when you pull in that will be clearly indicated. Then, there will be signs directing you to the location of the ceremony. Please do not drive up the path past the parking lot to the ceremony unless you have medical reasons to do so.


I still have questions, what’s the best way to get in touch?

Shoot us an email at wedding@tim-jennie.com
