COVID

We recognize the seriousness of the COVID-19 pandemic and want to make sure that we are able to celebrate in a safe way. However, we also understand that we don’t exactly know what the climate of it will be like in July. We are doing our best to follow all CDC and NYS guidelines for weddings. As these rules change, we will update this page. We will also send out an email leading up to the wedding with any changes you should be aware of. Additionally, if you don’t feel comfortable coming due to COVID, we completely understand and would never hold that against you!


Everyone needs to be tested before attending our wedding. NYS rules require a negative COVID test leading up to our wedding. Additionally, we expect all attendees to conduct a self screening for COVID symptoms. You may have to show proof of testing, we're not sure what that will look like yet. 


You may be required to quarantine in NY state if traveling from out of state. Currently the rules state that anyone traveling from a state that does not border NY must get tested before entering NY, quarantine upon entering the state, and get tested on day 4 of quarantine. More information on the NYS travel advisory can be found here. 


Masks will be required in the barn, and any time you are within 6 feet of others. Similar to a restaurant, masks will be required when getting up from your table, visiting others tables, dancing, etc. NYS considers our venue to be outdoors since the barn walls can be opened significantly (it becomes like a pavilion). Chairs for the ceremony will be spaced out to allow for 6 feet between parties. We know that it will be a warm summer day and are hopeful that NYS won’t require masks outside. 


The health department will be collecting information for contact tracing. We're waiting for more information from the health department about contact tracing. Currently, every attendee is required to sign in and provide some personal information (name, date of birth, and some contact info). We're not sure what this sign in process will look like yet.


If you get the full COVID vaccine more than 2 weeks before the wedding, you may be exempt from some requirements. Currently, vaccinated individuals are exempt from the testing requirement, and exempt from the travel quarantine requirements for 90 days after vaccination. We anticipate rules will be updated to allow for more exceptions as time goes on.
