FAQs

 

Can I use my phone at the ceremony?

We have paid a professional photographer to document our special day, and we would appreciate if her photos didn’t include your phone. (AKA, Tim will come find you if you try to take photos). Feel free to take as many photos as you’d like after the ceremony is over!

Can I bring a plus one? 

Your invitation will state how many seats we have reserved for your party. Weddings are expensive and we tried to give everyone that we know is in a committed, long-term relationship a plus one. If you feel that you should have gotten a plus one, reach out to us.

What’s the dress code? 

Summer casual. Our wedding will be outdoors and we’ll have lots of fun activities. We don’t want you to sweat through a suit!

Are kids welcome? 

If you know anything about Tim’s family, a good 75% of them couldn’t come if kids weren’t invited (especially Jared). Also, we love kids! Bring the little ones! There will be a playground, slides, and other fun things for them. Your invitation should have included them, if we miss counted how may kids you have or you somehow adopted several more in the past year….let us know…..MOM.

Where should I stay?

We haven’t reserved any hotel blocks, so we would recommend researching to find a place that fits both your desires and budget! Let us know if you have trouble finding somewhere.

Where are the ceremony and the reception taking place?

Both will be at Springside Farm. The Ceremony will be outside, and the Reception will be inside their beautiful barn (that does not have AC). The ceremony will be starting at 4pm sharp.

Are there any local places you’d recommend I check out while I’m in town?

If you’re looking for something indoors, you could check out the incredible mall, Destiny USA, or go to Turning Stone Casino. Otherwise, some of our personal favorite parks include Green Lakes, Chittenango Falls (in Jennie’s hometown!), or Highland Forest is pretty close to our venue!

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please RSVP by April 10th on our RSVP page. Let us know if you need more time or have trouble using the RSVP page.

I still have questions, what’s the best way to get in touch?

Shoot us an email at wedding@tim-jennie.com
