FAQs

 

What about COVID? 

We recognize the seriousness of the COVID-19 pandemic and want to make sure that we are able to celebrate in a safe way. Check out or COVID page for more info on how COVID affects our wedding.


Where should I stay?

We have a block of rooms reserved at Embassy Suites by Hilton Syracuse, you can reserve a room in that block using THIS LINK. If you’re looking for something different, there are multiple other hotels in that same area, just look up Carrier Circle on Google Maps and you’ll find a bunch. There are a limited number of Airbnb’s around as well.


Can I use my phone at the ceremony?

We would love it if you would turn off your phone/camera and enjoy the moment with us. We have paid a professional photographer to document our special day, and we would appreciate it if her photos didn’t include your phone. Feel free to take as many photos as you’d like once the ceremony is over!


What’s the dress code?

Summer casual. Our wedding will be outdoors and we’ll have lots of fun activities. We don’t want you to sweat through a suit!


Can I bring a plus one?

Your invitation will state how many seats we have reserved for your party. With the limitations due to COVID, we weren't able to give as many people a plus one as we would have liked do?


Are kids welcome?

If you know anything about Tim’s family, a good 75% of them couldn’t come if kids weren’t invited. Also, we love kids! Bring the little ones! There will be a playground, slides, and other fun activities for them. Your invitation should have included them, if we miss counted how may kids you have let us know!


Where are the ceremony and the reception taking place?

Both will be at Springside Farm - a Christmas Tree farm in Fabius, NY. The Ceremony will be outside, and the Reception will be inside their beautiful barn (that does not have A/C).


Are there any local places you’d recommend I check out while I’m in town?

If you’re looking for something indoors, you could check out the incredible mall, Destiny USA, or Turning Stone Casino. Otherwise, some of our personal favorite parks include Green Lakes, Chittenango Falls (in Jennie’s hometown!), or Highland Forest!


I still have questions, what’s the best way to get in touch?

Shoot us an email at wedding@tim-jennie.com
