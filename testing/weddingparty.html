Allie Cretaro
Maid of Honor

As Jennie’s best friend and travel companion, the two have gone on many adventures together. They first met when Jennie started going to CrossRoads Church her senior year of high school. Once Jennie joined the bible study Allie was a part of, they quickly hit it off. However, it wasn’t until Jennie and Allie almost got hit by a car going 50mph+ only to be saved by a trooper that their friendship was truly solidified. They have continued to get closer since Jennie went to college, including through a trip to Europe and another to Virginia together! Jennie is happy to have found such a great friend who won’t even get too mad when Jennie constantly makes fun of her for being short :P

Jackson Kuffel
Best Man

Though this may seem a bit unexpected, Tim and Jackson met in youth group when Tim was 15 and Jackson was 10. Their shared affection for film, computers and ice cream led the two to become quite close. At about the same time Tim went to college in Rochester, Jackson and his family moved to Buffalo - only an hour away. Since then, the two have spent weekends together running around Buffalo making short films or otherwise adventuring.

Maddy Deichman
Bridesmaid

Jennie met Maddy in her very first week at RIT, through their campus ministry, Cru. From the very first time they met up, the two knew they would be good friends. Maddy took on an unofficial mentor role for Jennie, especially in her first year of college. In fact, Maddy was one of the only people Jennie was truly honest with during the weird beginning to her relationship with Tim. She was always so supportive in sharing her wisdom and guiding her towards the Lord. It was on a trip to visit Maddy, and her then fiancé Josh, in Boston that Tim asked Jennie to be his girlfriend.

Josh Deichman
Groomsmen

Josh and Maddy are a bit of a funny story - they both became close with Tim and Jennie (respectively) before we even started being close friends, and they morphed into our mentor couple in the early stages after that. Josh was Tim’s bible study leader his freshman year when they first became friends. Since then the two have enjoyed spending time together nerding out about their shared love of computers and going on double dates with their girls.

Lauren Markley
Bridesmaid

They first got close through fun and goofy times at YL camp, which is where Lauren encouraged Jennie to try out CrossRoads. The Lord used Lauren to play an integral role in Jennie’s decision to accept Christ as her Savior. Lauren has continued to mentor and be a role model for Jennie but has also become a great friend. Lauren has been shipping Tim and Jennie together since before Jennie truly was...
